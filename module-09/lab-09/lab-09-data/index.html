Where do they power the USA?

Map authored by Hallie Liu

This map shows which power plants within the United States use coal, wind, or water as fuel sources. Circles denote plant location; size denotes power output in megawatts; and color indicates which fuel source is represented. Fuel source layers can be tobbled using the check boxes in the upper righthand corner. Click on a circle for more information about that plant; click on a blank space to limit the selection to power plants within a 500 km radius. Some plants may have multiple fuel sources. Power plant information courtesy of the EIA, and available here.
